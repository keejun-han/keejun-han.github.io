Keejun Han

keejun{AT}etri.re.kr

I am a full-time researcher in the Intelligent Convergence Research Laboratory at Electronics and Telecommunication Research Institute (ETRI), located in Daejeon, South Korea. Before Joining ETRI, I was a staff engineer (CL3) in the service S/W lab at Samsung Electronics. My research interests are Over-the-Top(OTT) Information Retrieval & Recommendation, and Neural Knowledge Graph Embedding. I obtained my MS and Ph.D advised by Mun. Y. Yi from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). I obtained my bachelor degree from the Australian National University (ANU), located in Canberra, Australia.
